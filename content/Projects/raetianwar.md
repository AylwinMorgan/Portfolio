## Raetian War
This is a personal project. It is the first few levels of a tactical strategy game. I made this game primarily to show off my ability to create strategic AI enemies.


![Character turn with movement visualizer](/Portfolio/images/raetia1.png)


Enemies will use the terrain in the levels to their advantage, seeking out defensive locations to fire at the player from.


![Enemy unit mid attack standing in a trench tile](/Portfolio/images/raetia2.png)
