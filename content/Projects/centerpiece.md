This is my centerpiece project for my portfolio which I am currently calling Homunculus Story as a placeholder title. Bear in mind that this game is still in development and the footage on this page will reflect that fact.

![Game title screen](/Portfolio/images/labescapetitle.png)
*Placeholder title screen*

This is a personal project of mine which I have been working on to demonstrate my AI and gameplay programming abilities. The game plays as a top-down 2D shooter with deckbuilding mechanics.

![Adding cards to deck](/Portfolio/images/labescapelevelup.png)
*The player can add cards to their deck between rooms*

After exploring through some semi-randomly generated rooms, the player finds themselves in a more difficult boss fight before they can leave the area and progress in the game.

![Normal room](/Portfolio/images/labescapeenemies.png)
*A normal, semi-random room. Layout is predetermined but enemies are random*

![Boss fight](/Portfolio/images/labescapebossfight.png)
*The boss is currently the end of the game, it will cause chunks of the ceiling to fall on the player*
